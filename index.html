Instructions

  1. To get started, click the "Agent List" button at the top of this page
  2. Find your name from the selection, set your availability, and wait for an incoming call
  3. When you leave, or step away from your desk, make sure to set youself as unavailable
  4. At the end of each call you will be placed back into the unavailable status, you MUST set yourself back to "Available" inorder to receive the next call
